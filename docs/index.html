Take Control of Your Music Collection

Deep Cuts is a private, offline-first music organizer and spatial mapping utility. Analyze, search, and catalog your local audio library using on-device machine learning and signal processing.

Designed for DJs & Sound Curators

Find the perfect transition, detect acoustic duplicates, and browse by vibe rather than rigid filenames.

💬

Search by Vibe & Feeling

Find tracks using descriptive queries like "dark atmospheric late-night techno" or "chill ambient background chords"—no manual tagging required.

🔥

Find Similar Sounding Tracks

Click "Sounds Similar" on any track to instantly scan your library for songs with matching acoustic energy, timbral characteristics, and rhythm.

⏱️

Accurate BPM & Key Detection

Prevent double-time or half-time timing errors. The pipeline double-checks temporal results against musical profiles and genre tags to ensure a precise grid.

Crafted for the macOS Minimalist

Ditch bloated, resource-heavy web wrappers and complex command line developer setups.

⚡

Snappy Svelte 5 & Rust Core

Built as a single-process Tauri 2 and native Rust application. Enjoy lightning-fast startup times and low memory footprints without running background Python services.

🛡️

Sandboxed System Safety

Runs fully sandboxed. Deep Cuts only reads files from watched directories you explicitly select, ensuring system security and privacy.

📈

Interactive Visual Tools

Visualize your collection with a 2D UMAP Music Map, plot relative loudness and mood spider charts, and compare audio statistical breakdowns in real time.

Built for the Sovereign Music Archivist

Preserve your local music library's integrity with absolute privacy and data sovereignty.

🔒

100% Offline Curation

No phone-home telemetry, trackers, or cloud dependencies. Your files and computed analysis metrics remain completely on your local machine.

💾

Non-Destructive Sidecars

Store your computed keys, BPMs, and genre labels in optional .dc.json sidecar files next to your audio. Your pristine media directory remains untouched, and re-indexing is near-instantaneous if you move folders.

📂

AcoustID Tag Enrichment

Automatically match missing album, artist, and release tags using AcoustID acoustic fingerprinting, and cache cover art locally without cluttering your filesystem.
