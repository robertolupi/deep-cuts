Advanced Audio Intelligence Features

Everything you need to index, query, study, and understand your local sound files—without external cloud APIs.

📊

Audio DSP Pipeline

Computes spectral-flux onset BPM, harmonic Krumhansl-Schmuckler key & scale profiles, precise integrated LUFS loudness (EBU R128), and builds beautiful 128-point RMS energy waveforms.

🧠

Essentia Classifiers

Leverages Discogs-Effnet ONNX models to extract vocal/instrumental separation ratios, 400 hierarchical genre classifications, and seven mood indicators (happy, aggressive, party, etc.).

🗺️

UMAP Music Map

Projects your entire collection into a zoomable 2D map via LAION CLAP acoustic embeddings. Discover sonic density clusters, scan popular genres, and locate similar tracks visually.

💬

Local Audio Chat

Have an interactive text session with any audio track. Ask questions about vocal placement, track structure, or mixing balance powered locally by Qwen2-Audio and llama-server.

⚡

Semantic & KNN Search

Use term-frequency hybrid search or vector matching to find tracks that sound acoustically similar or match natural language queries like "dark tech house base drop".

🔍

AcoustID & Sidecars

Acoustic fingerprinting automatically downloads missing tags and metadata. Store all computations in sidecar .dc.json files so your library analysis survives relocations.
